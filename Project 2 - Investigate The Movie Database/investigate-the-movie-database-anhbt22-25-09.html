  • Questions that I plan on exploring:
    1. Which genres are most popular from 2013 to 2015?
    2. 5 genres make the most profit from 2013 to 2015?

def plot_2d_barh(df_index, df, title, xlabel, ylabel,figsize=(15,10)):
    plt.subplots(figsize=figsize)
    plt.barh(df_index, df)
    plt.title(title)
    plt.xlabel(xlabel)
    plt.ylabel(ylabel);

Conclusions¶

  1. Drama is the genre most produce from 2013 to 2015.
  2. Adventure is the genre most popular from 2013 to 2015.
  3. Adventure also the genre with movies that have the most profit from 2013 to 2015.
  4. Documentary is the least popular genre from 2013 to 2015.
  5. 5 genres have the most profit from 2013 to 2015 accounts for more than half of the industry's profits from 2013 to 2015

Limitations¶

  1. We are working with sample data, which implies uncertainty (a different sample may lead to different results).
  2. We find issues with the sample of data that we are working with, the missing budget, missing revenue,,,.
